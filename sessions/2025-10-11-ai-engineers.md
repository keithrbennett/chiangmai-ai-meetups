# AI Engineers — October 11, 2025

## Anthropic & Claude Deep Dive

- **The Great Rate Limit Debate:** Anthropic shifted from daily to weekly usage caps. Power users on $100/month can burn through their entire weekly limit in 2-3 days. The $200 plan is now effectively mandatory for any serious power user.
- **The Decline of Opus:** Strong consensus that Claude 3 Opus is obsolete. Anthropic itself recommends Sonnet.
- **"Context Anxiety":** As the 200k token context window fills up, the model gets "anxious" and starts rushing. The 1M token experiment solved this — the model performed better with more "breathing room."

## OpenAI's Commerce Ecosystem & The Future of Shopping

- **From Search to Conversation:** Instead of searching on Amazon, have a detailed conversation with an agent about specific requirements.
- **The "Agent-to-Commerce" Protocol:** Includes structured conversations between buyer and seller agents, delegated payments, and product feeds.
- **The New SEO War — Data Pollution:** Companies generating hundreds of AI-generated, keyword-stuffed landing pages designed to rank in AI models. An SEO expert's own AI-generated article ranked #1 in Perplexity within days.
