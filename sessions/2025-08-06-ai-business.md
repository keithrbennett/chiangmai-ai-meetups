# AI Business — August 6, 2025

This week's discussion was a dynamic mix of high-level business strategy, practical AI development techniques, international business structures, and fascinating insights into niche global markets.

## AI-Driven Business Strategy & Market Validation

- **Ideation Funnel:** Use AI to brainstorm a wide range of ideas from a single "seed" concept. Fan out possibilities, rank them, then create a detailed PRD for the most promising one.
- **Synthetic Users for Market Research:** A company uses AI to create "synthetic users" — first conducting real surveys for baseline data, then creating AI simulations. If results match (3 out of 5 segments were accurate), the AI simulation can replace future costly real-world surveys.
- **"Vibe Code" a Landing Page First:** Don't even build an MVP. "Vibe code" a compelling landing page, collect emails, and use the signup form to gather data on what users actually want.
- **Building in Public:** Helps create a brand where the founder becomes the product. But it's stressful and poses massive security risks — one founder got hacked on day three. Solution: Build in a sandbox. Show progress publicly, but never the full production-ready codebase.

## Practical AI Tools & Sales Automation

- **The Sales Call Assistant:** Feed sales-focused books into an AI model with a system prompt for handling objections. During live calls, the system transcribes in real-time and provides instant responses based on expert knowledge.
- **The AI-Native Sales Pipeline:** Every sales call auto-transcribed to CRM + Drive folder. Before the next call, a bot creates a "cheat sheet." Monthly, another bot analyzes all intro calls for a performance report card. Plus a practice bot that simulates real customers.

## International Business & Incorporation Strategies

**European Options:**
- Romania & Bulgaria: Good EU options. Romania ~10.5% tax rate.
- Estonia: e-Residency program. Corporate tax only on distributed dividends. ~€1-2K annual maintenance.
- Slovakia: Not recommended (turnover-based tax).

**US Options (for Non-Residents):**
- Stripe Atlas: $500 for a US company (Delaware/Wyoming) + bank account.
- Mercury: Online-only bank popular with startups and VC funds.

## Niche Global Markets

- **Japan:** Trends spread like wildfire within communities due to social harmony. Prime market for SaaS and high-end vintage goods.
- **Vietnam & Vespas:** The 20-year trend of expats restoring junked Vespas and exporting to Europe.
